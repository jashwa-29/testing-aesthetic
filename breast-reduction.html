At The Aesthetic Studio Cosmetic and Plastic Surgery clinic in Chennai, we understand that heavy and large breasts can affect your daily life—physically, emotionally, and socially. If you're experiencing discomfort, back pain, posture issues, or want a more balanced figure, breast reduction surgery can help you feel lighter, confident, and comfortable in your body.

Performed by Dr. Bharathi Rajasridhar, a leading female plastic surgeon in Chennai, this procedure is customized to your body and lifestyle, ensuring natural-looking results with improved ease of movement and posture.

What Is Breast Reduction Surgery?

Breast reduction is a surgical procedure that extracts excessive fat, glandular tissue, and skin to reduce breast size proportionate to your body. We often meet patients who say, "I love the idea of feeling lighter and more confident!" If you have similar concerns, this surgery can help you relieve physical discomfort and improve your self-esteem.

Who Can Benefit from Breast Reduction?

You may be a good candidate for breast reduction if you:

  • Experience chronic back, neck, or shoulder pain
  • Feel limited in physical activity due to breast size
  • Trouble from skin irritation or rashes under the breasts
  • Struggle with posture issues or bra strap grooves
  • Feel self-conscious or emotionally affected by large breasts

The Breast Reduction Procedure

Consultation: As the first step, you will meet Dr. Bharathi, a Consultant plastic and cosmetic surgeon, to talk about your concerns, expectations, and medical history to understand the possible options and expected results.

Anesthesia: Breast reduction is carried out under complete general anesthesia. You will not perceive any pain during the procedure.

Incision: The plastic surgeon makes carefully planned incisions to remove excess tissue and reshape the breasts.

Reshaping: The breast size is reduced and reshaped to have a symmetrical appearance.

Incision Closure: The incisions with stitches are minimal and not visible.

Recovery : You will be monitored and guided on post-surgery care.

Recovery After Breast Reduction Surgery

Recovery is an essential part of your journey. After breast reduction, you will need some rest and proper care. Here's what you can expect:

  • Mild swelling and soreness for a few weeks.
  • Wearing a support bra to aid healing.
  • Avoid heavy activities for at least six weeks.
  • Following the plastic surgeon's instructions for optimal healing.

You will return to your previous daily activities within a few weeks and feel more comfortable and confident.

Key Benefits of Breast Reduction

This surgery can be life-changing for women who have dealt with large, uncomfortable breasts. Patients enjoy:

  • Improved comfort and mobility
  • Better posture and balance
  • Easier clothing fit and an active lifestyle
  • Relief from back, shoulder, and neck pain
  • Boost in self-confidence and emotional well-being

Risks and Considerations

Like any surgery, breast reduction surgery has potential risks, including:

  • Scarring (which fades over time)
  • Changes in nipple sensation
  • Difficulty breastfeeding (in some cases)
  • Infection or bleeding (rare)

You can always have an open discussion about these factors with our plastic surgeon.
