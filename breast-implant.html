Breast Implant Surgery: A Popular Choice

What Are Breast Implants?

Breast implants are silicone or saline-filled devices surgically placed into the breast tissue. This procedure is a key part of Breast Implant Surgery and helps achieve a fuller look.

Who is a Good Candidate for Breast Implants?

You may be an ideal candidate if you:

  • Feel your breasts are too small for your body frame.
  • Want to restore the size lost after weight loss, aging, or breastfeeding
  • Have asymmetrical or uneven breasts.
  • Are in a good state of health

Types of Breast Implants

Silicone Implants

These feel like natural breast tissue and are a common choice in breast enhancement surgery.

Saline Implants

These are inserted into the breast and filled with saline during the procedure.

Benefits of Breast Implant Surgery

  • Increases breast volume and symmetry
  • Long-lasting results
  • Enhances confidence and body image

Choosing the Right Procedure for You

At The Aesthetic Studio Cosmetic and Plastic Surgery Clinic in Chennai, we offer expert guidance in selecting the correct procedure for your needs. Breast implants may be the best choice for a significant increase in size.

Customized Surgical Approach

We understand that every woman's body is unique. That's why Dr. Bharathi will design a personalized surgical plan that considers:

  • Implant size and shape (round or anatomical)
  • Implant position – above or below the chest muscle
  • Incision placement – under the breast, around the nipple, or in the armpit
  • Skin tone and breast tissue condition

What to Expect – Procedure & Recovery

During the Procedure

  • Performed under general anesthesia
  • Typically takes 1 to 2 hours.
  • Daycare procedure—go home the same day, or may need to stay for 24 hours based on the doctor's advice

Recovery Timeline

  • You may experience mild discomfort, swelling, or tightness for the first few days.
  • Supportive surgical bra is recommended for 6-8 weeks
  • You can resume light activities in a week.
  • Full recovery is expected within 6–8 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Are breast implants secure?

    They are secure when performed by a qualified plastic surgeon using FDA-approved implants. The procedure has to be done in a sterile operation theater set up under all infection control precautions.

  • Will breast implants look and feel natural?

    Yes, especially silicone implants, which closely resemble natural breast tissue.

  • Is the recovery process long or painful?

    Recovery is short, and discomfort is minimal, usually improving within a few days.

  • Can I give breast milk after getting breast implants?

    Yes, most women can still breastfeed safely after the procedure.

  • How much does breast implant surgery cost in Chennai?

    The value of breast implant surgery costs differs depending on the plastic surgeon's skill and expertise, hospital facilities, and procedure complexity. During your Consultation, Dr Bharathi will provide expense breakdowns and discuss financing options if necessary.
