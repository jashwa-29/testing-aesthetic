What procedures does Dr. Bharathi specialize in?

Dr. Bharathi Raja Sridhar specializes in various cosmetic procedures to help you regain your confidence. Each treatment is carefully customized after understanding your needs and expectations to achieve clinical results.

Dr. Bharathi is an expert in:

  • Breast enhancement
  • Breast implants
  • Breast reduction
  • Gynecomastia surgery
  • Liposuction of Tummy, Thighs, Arms, Chin
  • Tummy tucks

Every procedure is about enhancing your natural beauty and confidence, not changing who you are.
