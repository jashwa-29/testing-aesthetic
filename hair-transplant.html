Regain Your Hair. Restore Your Confidence

Hair loss just doesn't change how you look; it affects how you feel. Whether it's a receding hairline, thinning crown, or bald patches, losing hair can take a toll on your self-esteem and confidence

At Dr. Bharathi's Aesthetic Studio, we understand how important hair is to your confidence and self-esteem. If you are struggling with hair loss, you are not alone! Many of our patients have regained their youthful appearance with our advanced hair transplant procedures.

What is a Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant is a minimally invasive method where healthy hair follicles are extracted (usually from the back of your scalp) and transplanted into bald or thinning areas. The result? Permanent, natural hair growth that looks and feels just like the rest of your hair. We use the latest advanced techniques like FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) for minimal downtime, precise placement, and fast healing.

Why Choose a Hair Transplant?

Hair transplant is a permanent and long-term solution. The hair from your donor area - usually from the back of the head/scalp area is transferred to bald areas. Our expert team ensures the procedure looks natural and enhances your overall appearance.

Our Hair Transplant Techniques

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)

  • Minimally invasive
  • No visible scars
  • Faster recovery

Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)

  • Ideal for larger bald areas
  • It provides a dense, natural look.
  • Long-lasting results

Direct Hair Implantation (DHI)

  • Advanced and precise Technique
  • Faster healing
  • High graft survival rate

Who is an Good Candidate?

You're ideal for a hair transplant if:

  • You have visible bald patches or thinning hair
  • You're looking for a permanent hair restoration option
  • You have healthy donor hair on the back or sides of your scalp

Cost of Chennai Hair Transplant

The cost of a hair transplant depends on several factors, including:

  • The Technique Used: FUE, FUT, and DHI have different pricing structures.
  • Number of Grafts Required: The more grafts needed, the higher the cost.
